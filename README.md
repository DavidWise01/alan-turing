# ALAN TURING · XI

> A sphere of **UD0** — the ROOT0 universe / biosphere. Domain: **LOGIKĒ**.

ALAN TURING (1912–1954) — LOGIKĒ XI, the man who gave logic a MACHINE. Gödel proved the formal dream had a ceiling; Turing, 23, asked what 'mechanically computable' even MEANS and answered with a device so simple it defines the word: ⚑ THE TURING MACHINE — a HEAD on an infinite TAPE of 0/1 cells, a STATE, and a tiny RULE TABLE ('in this state reading this symbol → write, move L/R, change state'). Nothing else — and it can compute anything computable at all ('On Computable Numbers', 1936). ⚑ THE UNIVERSAL MACHINE: one Turing machine can read the description of ANY other off its tape and simulate it → hardware & program become SEPARABLE = the idea of SOFTWARE and the general-purpose computer, on paper a decade before the electronics (von Neumann turned it into the stored-program architecture every computer still runs). ⚑ THE HALTING PROBLEM: no program H can decide for ALL programs whether they halt — proved by Gödel's diagonal made mechanical (build D that does the OPPOSITE of H's prediction about D(D) → H is always wrong → H can't exist). LIVE: (1) a RUNNING Turing machine — step/run a 3-state BUSY BEAVER (halts after writing 6 ones; its growth is literally uncomputable) or a BINARY INCREMENTER (7→8), with the head crawling the tape, live state/step/ones counters, and the active rule lighting up in the table (verified); (2) the HALTING DIAGONAL — pick H's prediction, watch D refute it on BOTH branches (verified contradiction either way). CONFIDENCE-TIERED: STRONG (the machine, universality, undecidability of halting — rigorous, foundational, the literal blueprint of the computer) / A-MODEL-NOT-THE-BRAIN (the Church–Turing thesis — this captures ALL effective computation — is overwhelmingly supported but a THESIS about an informal notion, not a theorem; 'machines can think' was his 1950 CONJECTURE/TEST, not proven) / OFTEN-MISREAD (⚠ 'undecidable' = no SINGLE method for EVERY case, NOT 'computers are weak'; the Turing test is a thought experiment, not a verdict on consciousness). ⚑ THE LOOP CLOSES: every processor in [[psephos-processors-domain]] IS a physical Turing machine; Leibniz's calculation → Boole's algebra → Gödel's ceiling → Turing's universal machine. ⚠ HONEST — THE MAN: his Bletchley Park Bombe broke German naval Enigma (credited with shortening WWII, saving many lives), kept secret for decades so he was never publicly honoured in life; in 1952, prosecuted under Britain's gross-indecency law for being gay, forced to choose prison or chemical castration (chose the hormone treatment, lost his clearance), dead of cyanide poisoning at 41 in 1954 (ruled suicide). UK apology 2009, royal pardon 2013, the 'Alan Turing law' pardoned thousands more. The man who defined the machine and helped win the war with one was destroyed by the state he saved — the logic stands, the injustice is named.
